📚 Reading Medical & Psychological Books

My passion rises from exploring medical and psychological texts, where I gain a broad insight about the medical field and psychology.

By reading these books, I hone my knowledge in neuroscience, human behaviour, and contemporary knowledge about these fields. Medical science was always my passion and aspiration since childhood. Moreover, psychological books have always amazed me.

My favorite books are The Alchemist, Atomic Habits, and Human Biology.

This reading not only broadens my knowledge but also inspires my volunteer work with Afghan women, as I apply psychological insights to support their resilience and growth amidst adversity.

♟️ Playing Chess & Strategic Mind Games

Chess playing and other mind games were always at the top of my interests. These games have drawn me to the game's demand for foresight, patience, and strategic adaptation.

Engaging in mind games strengthens my critical thinking and problem-solving skills — qualities I leverage in academic pursuits and community leadership.

Each match is a mental exercise that mirrors real-life challenges, teaching me to anticipate outcomes, remain composed under pressure, and turn complex situations into opportunities for thoughtful, calculated action.

💻 Coding & Technical Development

I devote time to coding in HTML, CSS, Python, and using tools like Termux, building both websites and small applications.

This technical hobby merges creativity with logic, allowing me to design functional, user-centered digital solutions.

Each project enhances my analytical abilities and introduces me to new frameworks, reinforcing my belief that technology — when coupled with purpose — can become a powerful medium for education and positive change in underserved communities.
